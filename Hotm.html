About Us

Welcome to OMFO MOMO, the most popular fast food spot in Shahabad, Uttar Pradesh. We are known for our delicious momos and pizzas that keep customers coming back for more.

Our restaurant features a comfortable dine-in area with a modern yellow and black interior, perfect for hanging out with friends or family. We pride ourselves on good taste, affordable prices, and friendly staff.
